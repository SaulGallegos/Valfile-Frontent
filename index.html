Why We Exist?

The job market is more saturated than it has ever been from utilizing in an outdated model.

Job market saturated leads to overwhelming amount of applications for recruiters to analyze per posting, which leads to human errors.

We shouldn’t be used to being ghosted by recruiters, yet we are due to the current unsustainable recruitment models.

What Now

We’ve invented decentralized recruitment. It’s not about how many candidates can apply to one job anymore, decentralized recruitment takes pools of candidates and jobs to match them based on qualification match index.

We created Valfile Candidate Vortex (VCV), a system to drive decentralized recruitment.

Putting The Human Back in HR

VCV is reducing sourcing exhausting faced by recruiters by reducing the busy work out of the process and letting them put the human back in Human Resources.

We aim to reduce job hunt exhaustion applying to the right jobs for you and letting you decide if you’d like to continue with the recruitment process if it’s a match.

We’re partnering with more companies each day to diversify our job pool.
